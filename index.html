High-frequency emotions — gratitude, love, joy — emit coherent electromagnetic signals from the heart (up to 5,000× the brain's signal). Feel the reality before it physically arrives.

The subconscious doesn't distinguish between vividly imagined and real. Create a mental movie of your desired reality with all five senses for 5–10 minutes daily, especially upon waking.

Quantum systems find the path of least resistance — over-efforting collapses possibility prematurely into limited outcomes.
